PRIVACY POLICY

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?  

We collect information from you when you register an account within our application.

Your Account Information. You provide your mobile phone number to create a Bunch account. You provide us the phone numbers in your mobile address book on a regular basis, including those of both the users of our Services and your other contacts. You confirm you are authorized to provide us such numbers. You may also add other information to your account, such as a profile name, profile picture, and status message.

Application Interactions.  Your likes, comments, post, images and page views are collected and used in order for the application to function and serve it's purpose of social interaction and photo collage making. Your followers and people you are following are collected in order to enable inviting within the application.


WHAT DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION FOR?  
Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways: 

To personalize your experience:  Your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs. 

To connect you with your friends:   In order for you to invite your friends outside of Bunch, we enable SMS messaging address book contacts so they can join your collage. This requires explicit permission by you the user to send an invite to a contact. We will never send a message to a contact without your permission.


HOW DO WE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information. 

We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.
